About Us

We are motorsport team that was founded by Universitas Indonesia students who are interest, talented, and innovative in automotive sector, especially in karts, electric motorcycle, and motorsports.

Our mission is what drives us to inspire students who interest and have potential in automotive by creating groundbreaking innovation, by create an inclusive environment and making positive impact in the world of motorsport.

UI Motorsport not only competes in the E-Shark ROK Cup, but also plans to develop advances in the Go-Kart field as a result of its participation. Desire to learn more about Go-Kart and how to make it work better.

Our Vision and Mission

Vision

To use UI Motorsport as a catalyst for the team to develop their character and apply their knowledge and skills in engineering to real life.

Mission

Utilize UIMS as a platform for Universitas Indonesia students to develop theoritical and practical knowledge in motorsport to be able to compete in both national and international stages.
